Overview

Enterprise AI bills are pulled two ways. Per-user licences (Copilot, Gemini, Claude) sit on flat prices — you pay per head, not per token. Meanwhile the raw API price of a token stopped falling in 2025 and is creeping back up, even as the energy per prompt has broadly fallen. The old "just pay for tokens" advantage is narrowing — the charts below show which side wins.
