Assumptions & conversion factors (edit to refine)

Formula: emissions = IT-energy(kWh) × PUE × grid intensity. Per-unit energy defaults are all-in facility figures where a comprehensive measurement exists (Gemini), so PUE defaults to 1.0 — raise it only if you switch a factor to an IT-only basis. Full sourcing in the table below.

Unit prices & per-seat intensity (used by Cost / Seats input modes)

Prices vary by contract, edition, model and FX — treat as starting points and overwrite with your own. Licensed seats (Copilot, Gemini seats) are billed per user regardless of usage, so seat cost tells you nothing about tokens; usage is instead estimated from messages-per-user-per-day.
